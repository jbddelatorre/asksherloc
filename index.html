Who is Sherloc?

During the course of our advisory work, we recognized that our competitive intelligence capabilities and ability to “make things make sense” were both extremely high.

We were able to interpret culture, connections, information, and situations for clients, helping to crystalize what these things meant and what to do next.

At that point, we knew we were onto something.

We then set out to find smart people that could help us to harness our “Human In The Loop” power, combining it with Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning and Data Science to increase our ability to help clients 1000x.

That’s when Sherloc™ was born.

Throughout our conversations with hundreds of professionals, we asked for their thoughts on Culture, Information, AI, Business Intelligence, and what they were getting from their organizations.

The feedback that we received was that it was difficult to simply make sense of the information that they were seeing, and to understand what they needed to do, where, and when.

Sherloc™ is designed to provide the roadmap of what you need to do, where, and when, in an easy to understand and actionable way with the help you need to implement.
